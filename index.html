About

What you think about us ?

In the challenging times of Covid-19 many schools were shut down to contain the virus but that led to loss of study for the students belonging to weaker sections of society because online lectures were not much efficient. Also many fraudulent companies scammed such families in name of providing study material at an unbearable price which is available for free over the internet. Since many children fall in their trap because they are not aware that it is available absolutely free on the Internet, our main aim is to create a website to meet all the educational needs of such children varying from topic wise lectures, practice problems to medium through which they can raise their queries/doubts on one single platform
